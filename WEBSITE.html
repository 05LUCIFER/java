MCSTM

Situated in the laps of Himalayas, Manas College of Science Technology & Management
has been established with the aim of promoting vocational and professional education
for the youth of Uttarakhand especially the remote district Pithoragarh
– as an initiative for providing vocational upliftment & job competencies on 75th anniversary of Independence Day
“Amrit Mahotsava” as desired by Hon’ble Prime Minister to take new initiatives by the people.

As we are in the mission of imparting the skill based education to the youth,
therefore all courses we offer are the Vocational (B.Voc.) courses.
The focus is always to keep our students relevant and updated with the latest happenings in the industry by providing them maximum industry exposure.
Certified Personality Development classes, from ILC Colorado USA, are integrated with all these courses.
